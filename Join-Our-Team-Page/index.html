Our values


We're passionate about our values and a great work culture.


Love


Kuda means love, and love is at the centre of everything
we do.



Kindness And
Empathy


Everyone we meet deserves our kindness. We empathise
with people so we can help them.





Respect


When we interact with people outside Kuda, we give them
the same respect we give our coworkers and ourselves.



Quality Over Quantity


We don’t believe in busywork and spinning wheels. When
we put out work, it’s the best quality we can muster.






Transparency


We don’t hide or attempt to embelish the truth about who
we are, what we believe and how we feel.




Customer First


We put customers’ issues ahead of everything else.
Nothing is right for us until we have made things right with
our customers.




Collaboration


The fastest way for us to build products and
features our customers love is to work together
as a team and with those customers.

Accountability &
Ownership


Kuda ia a workplace that promotes
leadership, and everyone is accountable for
the work they do.

Growth
